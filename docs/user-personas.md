## Common Characteristics Across Personas

### Shared Needs
1. **Ease of Use**: Intuitive interface regardless of technical background
2. **Quick Results**: Fast generation and iteration
3. **Quality Output**: Professional-sounding results
4. **Variety**: Access to multiple genres and styles
5. **Sharing Capabilities**: Easy export and sharing options

### Technical Requirements
1. **Progressive Disclosure**: Simple interface with advanced options available
2. **Mobile Responsiveness**: Access across all devices
3. **Cloud Storage**: Save and access creations anywhere
4. **Collaboration Features**: Share and collaborate on projects

### Behavioral Patterns
1. **Discovery-Driven**: Users explore through browsing and experimentation
2. **Goal-Oriented**: Specific output needs drive usage
3. **Social Sharing**: High likelihood of sharing interesting results
4. **Iterative Creation**: Multiple attempts to achieve desired results

## Design Implications

### Interface Design
- **Progressive Complexity**: Start simple, reveal advanced features as needed
- **Visual Feedback**: Clear indication of generation progress and results
- **Genre Navigation**: Easy browsing of different musical styles
- **Preset Management**: Save and recall favorite settings

### Feature Prioritization
1. **Core Generation**: High-quality AI music creation
2. **Style Variety**: Comprehensive genre and mood options
3. **Sharing Tools**: Export, download, and social sharing
4. **Discovery**: Browse and explore generated content
5. **Personalization**: User preferences and history
